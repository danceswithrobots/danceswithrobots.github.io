Dances with Robots explores interdisciplinary collaborations at the intersection of choreography and technology, uncovering new insights into how these fields connect.

Season Two, coming July 2026

Season Two of Dances with Robots explores the people, ideas, and questions shaping humanity's future beyond Earth. Hosted by Ariane Michaud and Sydney Skybetter, the season brings together artists, scientists, astronauts, engineers, architects, and researchers to examine what it means to live, move, create, and build culture in space.

Through conversations about microgravity, access, ethics, design, and imagination, the season asks a simple question: Who gets to shape the future? And what happens when artists are invited into the room?

Dances with Robots is produced by the Conference for Research on Choreographic Interfaces (CRCI), housed at the Brown Arts Institute, in collaboration with Consciously Produced.
