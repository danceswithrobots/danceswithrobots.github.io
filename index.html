About the Podcast: Dances with Robots

The Conference for Research on Choreographic Interfaces (CRCI) presents Dances with Robots: a podcast catalyzed by the corporeal risks, opportunities and awkwardness of today’s modern technologies. Join our highly caffeinated facilitator and host, Brown Dean Sydney Skybetter, alongside a series of guest hosts, as we explore the ways in which artists, activists and technologists thrive through collaboration and interdisciplinary practice.

DWR was created by the CRCI Team in-place of an annual gathering. While we are not ready to gather in large numbers again yet, we hope that this podcast will continue to expand upon the conversations and community that we fostered through our conferences.
